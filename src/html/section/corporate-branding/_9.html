FAQ

How to create a strong corporate brand?

Corporate branding is a process of building a strong and identifiable identity essential for a company's growth. It includes everything from the company's mission, vision, values, and positioning to its name, logo and the visual elements. It allows companies to create a powerful presence in the market, stand out from competitors, build trust and loyalty of customers, and thus increase business profitability.

How to create a strong corporate brand?

Creating a strong corporate brand requires several key steps. These encompasses defining your brand identity (mission, values, and unique selling proposition), having a compelling brand story (brand’s history, purpose and vision), comprehending your target audience (market research), developing a distinctive visual identity (logo, fonts, imagery, brand guidelines), being consistent across all brand touchpoints (your website, social media profiles, marketing materials, packaging…), building your brand credibility and trust, etc. By implementing these steps, you can create a strong corporate brand that resonates with your target audience and drives long-term success.

How much does corporate branding cost?

The cost of corporate branding is calculated individually depending on specific requirements and the scope of the branding project: the industry, the general strategy, and the quantity of branding elements required.

How often should I update my corporate brand?

The frequency of updating or refreshing your corporate brand can vary depending on several factors such as market trends, changes in consumer preferences, business growth, and competitive dynamics. As a general guideline, it's advisable to assess your brand periodically, particularly when there are significant shifts in the market landscape or emerging trends that could impact your brand's relevance or competitiveness. Consider updating your brand identity as your business evolves to ensure alignment with its objectives and values. It’s highly important to monitor market changes and update your brand as needed to maintain its effectiveness and relevance.
