Brand Identity Development

STRATEGIZING

Step 1: BEGINNING

Before the project officially begins, we’ll discuss your goals and troubleshoot problems in order to get a good idea of what will work best for you.

Step 2: BRANDING QUESTIONNAIRE

You’ll answer questions that will help us to deeper understand your company’s values, objectives, and brand aspirations.

Step 3: BRANDING SESSION

1:1 branding session where we’ll go through your answers to the questionnaire in detail

DESIGNING

Step 4: LOGO DEVELOPMENT

Our designer will create a moodboard, i.e. visual presentation of images to make sure that we captured your needs correctly. We then develop two unique logo versions for your choice.

Step 5: MARKETING MATERIALS

We develop marketing materials depending on your needs (business card, invoice, email signature, social media banners, etc.) A wide choice of marketing materials are available on demand.

IMPLEMENTING

Step 6: GO LIVE

We hop on a final call where we present you the brand guide and all the final files. You are now ready to go live!

The package is fully customized.
