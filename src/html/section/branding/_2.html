Relevé

corporate branding

The main message of the brand is "Everything starts with our energy and emotions". We were asked to develop a corporate identity for the RELEVÉ brand, which would be fully in line with the ideology of the brand, reflecting the company's philosophy through visuals. Behind the RELEVÉ product is a whole philosophy about thinking mastery, about the fact that a person determines his or her own life. "my energy - my thoughts - my actions."

Through the corporate style we tried to convey the idea of the brand about emotional connection with clients, individual approach and convey it through respectability, solidity, premium segment.
